A little bit about me

Education was my first career, web development is my new love. I use my prior skills: editing, design, language, and problem-solving, plus updated coding techniques. My goal is to work with a team of dedicated professionals to create websites that are educational and engaging for modern learners.

I find satisfaction in the detail and precision of coding, much as in fiber artistry. Website creation is like weaving: it's all about knowing clearly what you want and then spinning the fibers to get just the right yarn, and then weaving the pattern that works. I strive to create a finished product that combines strength, utility, and beauty -- in fiber and in code.

I am quick to learn new skills, precise and concise in my language, hard-working and dedicated to excellence. I'm an enthusiastic team player -- and can't wait to help you score your internet goals!
