A little bit about me

I am an American, native English-speaker living in Israel. Education was my first career; web development is my new love. I exercise my prior skills from teaching: characterizing and motivating a target audience, crafting and editing presentations, and problem-solving. Now I have added new and updated coding techniques to that skillset. My goal is to work with a team of dedicated professionals to create websites that are educational and engaging for modern learners.

I find satisfaction in the detail and precision of coding, much as in my hobby of fiber artistry. Website creation is like weaving: it's all about knowing clearly what you want and then spinning the fibers to get just the right yarn, and then weaving the pattern that works. I strive to create a finished product that combines strength, utility, and beauty -- in fiber and in code.

How can I contribute? I am:

  1. 1. quick to learn new skills

  2. 2. precise and concise in my language and coding

  3. 3. hard-working and dedicated to excellence

  4. 4. an enthusiastic team player -- and I can't wait to help you score your internet goals!
